MediService - A Medical Application


MediService built in 2024 aims at solving the real world problems in health care industry by digitalization of all the services


An application to manage medical equipment within healthcare facilities streamlines inventory tracking, maintenance schedules, and procurement processes. It allows users to monitor equipment utilization, automate maintenance reminders, track repairs, and ensure regulatory compliance. Additionally, it can facilitate data-driven decision-making, optimize equipment allocation, and enhance overall operational efficiency within the healthcare environment.
